FLEXIBLE VOLUNTEERING

Volunteerism is a powerful force that can significantly enhance the impact of Compassionate Hands Community Initiative (CHCI) by bringing in diverse skills, experiences, and perspectives from individuals around the world. By engaging volunteers, CHCI can expand its reach, improve program delivery, and foster a sense of global solidarity and compassion. By incorporating volunteerism and engaging volunteers from around the world, Compassionate Hands Community Initiative (CHCI) can significantly enhance its capacity to serve the vulnerable populations in our communities.

Volunteers bring diverse skills, passion, and perspectives that can drive forward the mission of CHCI, fostering a global community united by compassion and faith. CHCI intends to create a structured volunteer program that outlines opportunities, roles, and responsibilities for both local and international volunteers. Develop a comprehensive volunteer handbook that includes organizational policies, expectations, and guidelines.
