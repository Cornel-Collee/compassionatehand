About Compassionate Hands Community Initiative

Compassionate Hands Community Initiative (CHCI) is a faith-based, non-profit organization founded in 2020 in Kochia, Homa Bay County, Kenya. Rooted in Christian values and teachings, CHCI is committed to serving the most vulnerable members of our community. We are inspired by the love and compassion of Jesus Christ, and our organization operates on the biblical mandate to care for widows, orphans, and the poor.

CHCI was established by a group of dedicated individuals who recognized the struggles faced by elderly widows, orphans, vulnerable children, and unemployed youth in rural communities. Our mission is to bring hope, support, and empowerment to these groups through sustainable, comprehensive programs aimed at improving their lives.

At CHCI, we believe that true faith is demonstrated through action, and we are driven by our vision to be a beacon of hope and love in rural communities. Through our programs for widows, youth, and vulnerable children, we strive to create a community where everyone can experience the compassion and care that Jesus taught us. We aim to make a lasting impact by following biblical principles and exemplifying Christ-like love.

Our Community Impact

Our programs reach some of the most disadvantaged individuals and communities in Homa Bay County and beyond. By empowering women, providing educational opportunities for children, and offering support for vulnerable youth, we are contributing to the overall well-being of rural communities. Our initiatives include:

  • Support for elderly widows
  • Educational scholarships for vulnerable children
  • Empowerment programs for unemployed youth
  • Health and wellness initiatives for marginalized communities

Get Involved

Compassionate Hands Community Initiative offers numerous ways for individuals and organizations to get involved.
